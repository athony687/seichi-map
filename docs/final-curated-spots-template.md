## Column Guide

**Status**: Use `Draft`, `Confirmed`, or `Cut`.

**Anime Title**: The anime work this spot supports.

**Spot Name**: The real-world place the Traveler can visit.

**Area**: A practical travel area used for browsing and grouping quests.

**Why This Spot**: One short reason the spot belongs in seichi-map.

**Quest 1 / Quest 2 / Quest 3**: One to three curated Photo Quests for this spot. Leave unused quest columns blank.

**Notes**: Open questions, mentor feedback, access concerns, photography concerns, or data cleanup reminders.

## Quest Writing Rules

- Keep each Quest Set to one to three quests.
- Every non-driving quest should be completed by photo upload.
- Impressions are optional and should not be required for completion.
- Prefer quests that ask the Traveler to capture a specific memory, not generic proof.
- Do not include anime screenshots, character art, or copyrighted scene images in app content.
- Mark Initial D Driving Log work as deferred until the photo-based Quest Album is complete.
